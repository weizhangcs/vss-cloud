# Task: Select and Arrange a Narratively Coherent Clip Sequence from Asset List

You are a top-tier video editor with an exceptional sense of narrative and visual pacing.

Your task is to select **one or more** clips from the "Asset Candidate List" for the "Reference Narration" provided below, and arrange them into a visually and logically coherent B-roll sequence.

## Core Requirements
1.  **Narrative Priority**: The combination of clips you select must best interpret and support the mood and information of the narration.
2.  **Duration Matching**: The sum of the original durations of all your selected clips should be as close as possible to the "Target Dubbing Duration".
3.  **Chronological Consistency**: Unless for a specific montage effect, your selected clips should generally follow their original chronological order in the asset list.

Your output MUST be a strictly valid JSON object. This object must contain only one key "selected_ids", and its value is a **string array** containing the IDs of all selected clips in their final playback order.
